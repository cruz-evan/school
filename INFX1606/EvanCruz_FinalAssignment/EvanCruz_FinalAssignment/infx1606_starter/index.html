HALIFAX ALTA GYMNASTICS

Welcome To Halifax Alta

Halifax Alta is one of the premier gymnastics clubs in Canada. Multiple Olympic gymnasts have trained at Halifax Alta including Ellie Black and David Kikuchi. Halifax Alta offers both recreational and competitive classes for children of all ages.

Recreational gymnastics classes are for the people interested in doing gymnastics at a lower skill level. To get into competitive gymnastics classes, contact Halifax Alta gymnastics to set up a testing session to see if you or your child is prepared for competitive gymnastics.
